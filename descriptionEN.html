Novalnet Payment Plugin V12.4.0

Novalnet Payment Plugin for Shopware 5 is an end-to-end solution for full-service processing of international and local payments around the world. By connecting to the Novalnet platform, it ensures that your transactions are protected by the highest level of security. To this end, Novalnet AG is audited and certified annually in a comprehensive security audit both by BaFin and according to the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) at the highest level 1.
